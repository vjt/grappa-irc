  grappa emits the right mime" (vjt). Threaded through every door

  regenerated.
- **cic audio category** (`46ad0b1`). Exhaustive `Record<UploadCategory>`
  made tsc enforce the mirror — `bun run build` flagged every surface
  grep missed (incl. the `userTopic.ts` WS narrower that would've dropped
  `audio_per_file_cap_bytes`). AdminSettingsTab gained a 4th cap row.
- **mediaLink 🎵** (`5a61975`) — `🎵 <slug-url>` classifies as audio.
- **Docked mini-player** (`79519fc`) — `AudioMiniPlayer.tsx` +
  `audioPlayer.ts` store (mirror of mediaViewer.ts). Non-modal, above
  compose, one `<audio>` singleton, swaps source on new click. vjt-
  approved (brainstorm): play/pause + scrubber + time + close. Mounted
  inside the `kindHasScrollback` Match → survives channel↔query↔server
  switches; leaving chat stops playback (acceptable v1).

  `normalizeUploadFile` re-labels by extension at `triggerUpload`
  (mirror of `@audio_ext_canonical_mime`). Also covers octet-stream .m4a.
